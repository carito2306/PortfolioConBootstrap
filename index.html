About me

Hello there!

I'm Carolina, graduated as a lawyer after much study at the Faculty of Law of the National University of Córdoba (UNC). I began my judicial career in 2014 after passing the entrance exams for the Judiciary, but during 2020 something changed.
That year I was granted unpaid leave to carry out an investigation into the electronic file in Spain, but due to the well-known pandemic I had to rearrange plans. In those months of quarantine I took my first Graphic Design course, which led me to learn about UX/UI Design and hence Web Development, falling deeply in love with FRONT END DEVELOPMENT.
It was the beginning of a radical change in my working life. So I continued training to date and working as a freelance on different projects.
